Offering a restaurant, Tayrona Tented Lodge is located 1 hour and 15 minutes’ drive from Santa Marta. It offers a 24-hour front desk service.

Each functional bungalow has a rustic-style and will provide you with a balcony. Featuring a shower, the private bathroom also comes with free toiletries. You can enjoy the sea views from the room. Extras include a fan.

At Tayrona Tented Lodge you will find a garden. The property offers free parking.

The property offers its guests continental and American-style breakfast.

The hotel is 7 km from Tayrona Park and 38 km from Santa Marta National Park.
