    Boutique Hotel on San Francisco's Embarcadero

    Relax and rejuvenate while enjoying stunning views of the Embarcadero waterfront and downtown city vistas, penthouse level spa with rooftop soaking tubs and signature Americano Restaurant & Bar. It's time for some "you" time.

    See Top Reasons to Stay
    An Urban Location That Feels Close to Nature

    Hotel Vitale combines the natural beauty of its waterfront location on the Embarcadero with the modern luxury of San Francisco’s Downtown. Unwind on our unique boutique private terraces with gorgeous views of the iconic Bay Bridge with its spectacular Bay Lights, and when you are ready to explore, stroll across the street to the San Francisco historic Ferry Building, a gourmet mecca or walk to some of the citys best restaurants, world renowned shopping and attractions.

    A Revitalizing Luxury Retreat

    Leave your worries at the door. We welcome you with lavender-scented hand towels at check-in and lavender sprigs in the hallways. Treat yourself to transcendent services at Spa Vitale and delicious cuisine at Americano Restaurantall without leaving the hotel.

    Personal, Approachable Service

    We do everything we can to make your stay more relaxing and carefree. Consult our concierge for the best dining and entertainment recommendations, stay centered with complimentary daily yoga and enjoy lounging in your room with room service and complimentary Wi-Fi. And for the savvybusiness traveler, we offer daily shoe shines and morning town car service to the Financial District.
