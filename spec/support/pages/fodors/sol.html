    Sol de Napoles

    • Calle 69 No. 11–58 Map It
    • Bogotá
    • Italian

    Updated 06/22/2011

    Fodor's Review

    Family recipes and fresh bread make this reasonably priced eatery a longtime favorite among Bogotanos. Try pasta topped with one of the sauces—the meaty bolognesa and the spicy arrabiata are favorites, but even the "plain" is enormously satisfying.

    Italian
